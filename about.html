About Us

Hounds at Home is a Tampa local business focused on in-home pet care. We are a fully licensed, bonded and insured company, able to do business all throughout the Sunshine State. We are very hands-on, with extensive required training for our sitters, who are taught by the owners of the company first hand. Our sitters are always happy, and in turn want to keep your pets happy.

Happiness for your animals is our goal, and we find they are as happy as can be when they are @Home.
