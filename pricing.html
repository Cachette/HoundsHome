Pricing & Availability

Base Pricing

We're no strangers to the outrageous prices that can be seen when trying to find care for your animals. That's why Hounds@Home tries its best to keep the prices affordable, whether you're retired and secure or a struggling student.

Holidays & Distance

Around the holidays, a lot of people still have to work. This includes the dedicated pet sitters of Hounds@Home! Not only are they working, they aren't even in their own homes! So as a way of giving back to them, we charge a little bit extra during those times. Additionally, there is an extra charge if you are more than 10 miles from your sitter. Keep in mind that they may have to drive to and from your home up to three times a day!
